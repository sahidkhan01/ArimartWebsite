About Us

we( ARIMART) are trying to optimizing the supply chain system ,which is inefficient and multi-layered . After various round of price marked up across multiple intermediaries , Price of the products got quadrupled before it reach to the end consumers. ARIMART dedicate itself to promoting the efficiency of traditional agricultural supply chain in india . Farmers can now sell their produce In the Bulk ,but at the retail price, directly to the consumers through our Arimart platform. By our Group aggregation model , ARIMART can help aggregate large volume of orders in shorter period of time ...Bypassing unnecessary layers of intermediaries , farmers benefits from significant increase in their income ,while consumers on our platform also enjoy cheaper and better quality ...Also we can significantly reduce the wastages of the products that is close to 40% (due to shorter life -span).
